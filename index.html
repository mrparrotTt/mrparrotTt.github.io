About the Flags

Every flag is handmade from wood and finished with a bold American flag design. Wavy flags have a carved look that gives the flag movement and depth. Flat flags have a cleaner, traditional look.

Custom designs are available. If a design is too detailed to be made directly in the wood, it may be added as a sticker.
